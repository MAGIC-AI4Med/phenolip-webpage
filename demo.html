PhenoKG — Phenotype Knowledge Graph

PhenoKG organizes phenotypes along the Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) is_a hierarchy. The top-level anatomical systems below are sized by the number of descendant phenotypes and shaded by how many PhenoBench images they cover. Use the explorer to expand the is_a subtree of any phenotype.

Anatomical Systems

Top-level systems under Phenotypic abnormality (HP:0000118). Box area = number of descendant phenotypes; color = number of PhenoBench image–caption pairs covered by that system.
